# Sample Questions and Answers

## Question 1

**What is BERT?**

### Expected Answer

BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) is a language representation model introduced by Google. It uses bidirectional training of Transformers to better understand the context of words in a sentence.

---

## Question 2

**What is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)?**

### Expected Answer

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines semantic retrieval with a Large Language Model. Instead of relying only on the model's internal knowledge, relevant document chunks are retrieved first and then provided to the LLM for grounded answer generation.

---

## Question 3

**Explain self-attention.**

### Expected Answer

Self-attention enables each token in a sequence to attend to every other token, allowing the Transformer to capture contextual relationships efficiently.
